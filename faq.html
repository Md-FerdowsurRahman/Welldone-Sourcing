FAQ

MATERIAL SOURCING:

Are the entire material source by locally?

Yes.

Have you good source from abroad?

Yes, if required.

Are Yarn source from locally?

Yes.

If fancy yarn, where you source from?

If we source locally, if not then preferably go to China.

From where are you collect all of the accessories sourcing?

Mostly from local supplier, if not then 1st preference to China.

If it is fancy accessories, where you source from?

Such this case we collect it from China source.

SAMPLING:

Do you have separate sample section for development?

Yes.

How many days for sampling lead-time by local yarn?

7 days maximum.

How many days for sampling lead-time by Imported yarn?

21 days maximum.

Are the sample designers are well understand about buyer Tech pack?

Yes.

Are all the samples checked by sample QC before sending to customer?

Yes, It must checked before sending to customer.

PRODUCTION:

Are the production floor have all in house facility?

Yes, this is from knitting to finishing.

Where you do embroidery?

Where you do Print?

Such case we took support from other good factories.

Where you do wash, like PC DYE/ Acid wash?

Such case we took support from other good factories.

How many days you required for production lead-time by imported yarn of Trial order?

120 days for imported yarn from order confirmations date.

How many days you required for production lead-time by imported yarn for REPEAT order?

90 days for imported yarn from order confirmations date.

How many days you required for production lead-time by Local yarn for trial order?

90 days for Local yarn from order confirmations date.

How many days you required for production lead-time by Local yarn for REPEAT order?

60 days for Local yarn from order confirmations date.

Can you perform TRIAL order 60 days by Local yarn/accessories?

Yes, we can do if buyer request.
